Camp rules

  • Septic & water: We're on a well and septic system. Keep showers short, use the outdoor hot shower, and feel free to pee in the woods.
  • Speed limit: 15 mph on the dirt road — the neighbors are intense about dust.
  • Food storage: Keep coolers sealed (bring a bungee if yours doesn't latch). Bring ice — the fridge will be bursting. There's pantry space in the house.
